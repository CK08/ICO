9         !-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
10         ! English::
11         ! The following global variables have different functions in the main program, such as:         
12         !        - RToler (Convergence Residual Tolerance)
13         !        - nds (Number of dimensions in the problem)
14         !        - incmax (Maximum number of increments in the analysis)
15         !        - itermax (Maximum number of iterations per increment in the analysis)
16         !        - p, q, w (degree in each direction: x, y e z, respectively)        
17         !        - ncpx, ncpy, ncpz (Number of control points in each direction - x, y e z, respectively)
18         !        - nnodes (Number of nodes)
19         !        - nelems (Number of elements)
20         !        - nshpl (Number of shape functions)
21         !        - closed_u, closed_v, closed_w (Closed Knot Vectors - Defined as in IGA book (Hughes) but currently NOT IN USE!)
22         !        - elcode (String with element type, for example: 'Hex8')
23         !        - nbc (Number of boundary conditions)
24         !        - ndisp (Number of degrees of freedom affected by prescribied displacements)
25         !
26         !        - npi (Number of integration points per element)
27         !        - SEnergy (Strain Energy)
28         !        - SEnergyConv (Strain Energy Converged)
29         !        - props (Material properties vector. The order of the vector is given in table 1 of ICO User Manual)
30         !        - iprops (Dimension of "props" vector)
31         !        - npenal (Penalty Method applied to DupNode [Duplicated Nodes] - in Assembly3D.f90)
32         !        - ipenal (Penalty Method applied to DupNode [Duplicated Nodes] - in Assembly3D.f90)
33         !        - bc (Boundary Conditions, not for prescribed displacements)
34         !        - iper ("Nao me lembro" - J.F. Caseiro)
35         !        - dir_bc (not used)
36         !        - ld_el (not used)
37         !        - load (Matrix with point loads)
38         !        - dispBC (Prescribed Displacement Boundary Conditions)
39         !        - BW (Weighted control points)
40         !        - EY (not used - possibly assign one material for each element [EY - Young modulus])
41         !        - NU (not used - possibly assign one material for each element [NU - Poisson's coefficient])
42         !        - Fext (External Forces)
43         !        - FextEq (External Forces Reduced System)
44         !        - Fini (Initial Forces)
45         !        - FInc (Incremental external force)
46         !        - Fint (Internal Forces)
47         !        - Res (External Forces - Internal Forces = Residual)
48         !        - ImpDisp (Implicit/Prescribed Displacement - Array)
49         !        - Kf (Global Stiffeness Matrix)
50         !        - KfEq (Reduced Global Stiffeness Matrix)
51         !        - KfEqInv (Inverse of the Reduced Global Stiffeness Matrix - currently not used [slowwww])
52         !        - dispeqv (Increment of the Increment of the Reduced Displacement)
53         !        - RedCount (Auxiliar array to construct the global displacement array from the reduced one)
54         !        - dDisp (Displacement Increment)
55         !        - ddDisp (Incremente of the displacement increment)
56         !        - u (Displacement)
57         !        - stress (STRESS:: element number, integration point, stress component)
58         !        - dstress (Stress Increment)
59         !        - stress_conv (Converged Stress)
60         !        - strain (Strain:: equal to stress)
61         !        - dstrain (Strain Increment)
62         !        - strain_conv (Converged Strain)
63         !        - hard (Hardening:: element number, integration point)
64         !        - hard_conv (Converged Hardening)
65         !        - TmatD (Constitutive (D) matrix backup)
66         !        - laxis (local axis:: element, integration point, axis)
67         !        - laxisconv (Converged local axis)
68         !        - PMesh (Control Points Coordinates in the global numbering system [i.e. GiD, contact])
69         !        - stpfrac (Fraction of applied prescribed displacement in the increment)
70         !        - Weight (Control Point Weights)
71         !        - Points (Control Points Coordinates)
72         !        - penalmeth (Penalty Method - it does something cool!!!)
73         !        - nlgeom (bool: if True - accounts for GNL [Geometric Non-Linearity])
74         !        - loaddof (Load with global numbering)
75         !        - dispdof (Zero displacement BC with global numbering)
76         !        - Eigen (Used to compute Eigen values of a 3x3 matrix)
77         !        - vcp (Eigen Vectors)
78         !        - vlp (Eigen Values) 
79         !        
80         !        MATERIALS - material properties relative dimensions 
81         !        - ndi (Number of direct components) 
82         !        - nshr (Number of Shear components)
83         !        - ntens (ndi + nshr)
84         !        
85         !        KNOT VECTORS - 
86         !        - u_knot, v_knot, w_knot (knot vectors in each direction: x, y e z, respectively)
87         !        
88         !        CONTROL POINTS - 
89         !        - b_net (Control Points original coordenates matrix)
90         !        - b_net_final (Control Points deformed coordenates matrix) 
91         !        
92         !        NURBS COORDENATES VECTORES 
93         !        - INN (basis function for a determined connectivity/element)
94         !
95         !        NODES PER ELEMENT VECTORS - 
96         !        - IEN (Matrix with the connectivities for IG [IsoGeometric] elements - are given by the knot vectors, first in the x direction, followed by y and z)
97         !        - conn ("Nao serve para nada" - J.F. Caseiro)
98         !
99         !        GRAVITY -
100         !        - gravity (Bool - if TRUE, gravity is considered in the analysis)
101         !        - gravdir (Integer that takes values 1, 2 and 3 for gravity direction: x, y ou z, respectively)
102         !        - gconst (Gravitacional Constant "g")
103         !
104         !        PRESSURE - 
105         !        - ipress (Integer that defines the number of surfaces under pressure load)
106         !        - ierror (error index)
107         !        - tempc (read file - not important or is it?)
108         !        - pressvec (Pressure Magnitude)
109         !        - ftag (Element Face - read ICO User Manual)
110         !        - pressure (Bool - True if pressure exists)
111         !                
112         !        B-BAR METHOD - 
113         !        - IENb (Connectivities for B-Bar)
114         !        - MatAf (B-Bar mixed method - zienkiewicz book [good luck ;)])
115         !        - MatVf (B-Bar mixed method - zienkiewicz book [good luck ;)])
116         !        - MatCf, MatCfT (B-Bar mixed method - zienkiewicz book [good luck ;)])
117         !        - Kbar (Stiffeness after static condensation of B-Bar)
118         !        - fbar (Load after static condensation of B-Bar)
119         !        - nalpha (number of EAS enhanced parameters)
120         !        - diag ()
121         !        - nlin, ncol (Number of line and columns of the MatVf)
122         !        - indx (index number)    
123         !    
124         ! Added by Diogo Cardoso, March 2014.
